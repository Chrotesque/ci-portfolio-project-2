Help

Simon Game is a simple game of memorizing the order of buttons pressed by the computer, then correctly repeating the order yourself.

The game consists of turns, sequences and rounds.

Both you and the computer take turns to press the game buttons.

The order in which the buttons are pressed is the sequence which grows with each turn but remains the same until the end of the round.

Once the player has replicated the entire sequence correctly, the round ends and a new one starts.

Should the player make a mistake, the game is either over OR the previous computer turn is repeated - depending on the difficulty.

There are 4 difficulty levels:

  • Easy - The sequences are played at the slowest speed, you have to deal with 3 buttons and can repeat a turn upon making a mistake.
  • Normal - The sequences are played at the standard speed, you have to deal with 4 buttons and can repeat a turn upon making a mistake.
  • Hard - The sequences are played faster, you have 6 buttons and a single mistake will end the game.
  • Custom - This is where we leave the phrase "simple game" behind and the settings menu comes into full play, allowing for incremental changes to suit your needs and wants.
